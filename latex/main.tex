\begin{abstract}
This submission presents a framework for transforming Australian domestic energy 
consumption through integrated efficiency measures and circular economy principles.
\end{abstract}

\section{Executive Summary}

Australia faces a critical juncture: fuel excise revenue is declining as vehicle 
electrification accelerates. This framework shows how targeted investments can 
generate economic multipliers of 4.35x.

\section{Key Recommendations}

\begin{itemize}
    \item Waste heat recovery in buildings
    \item Battery swapping infrastructure
    \item Vape battery recycling integration
    \item Economic multiplier: 4.35x per subsidy dollar
\end{itemize}

\section{Economic Impact}

\begin{itemize}
    \item Fuel excise replacement: 114\%
    \item Jobs created: 100,000 by 2030
    \item Energy reduction: 35-45\%
\end{itemize}
